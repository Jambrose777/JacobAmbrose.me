Cubing

I solve twisty puzzles (like Rubik's Cubes) competitively. My fastest official time is 7.87 seconds. I've attended over 170 competitions and have more than 9,200 offical solves. I specialize in other events like Blindfolded solving, finding solutions in the fewest moves, and Speed Solving the Rubik's Clock. I was ranked 6th in the World in the Clock Event. In 2017 I attended 45 competitions in a year and acheieved 2,675 offical solves in 1 year, both of which were world records. You can read more about my competive solving here.
