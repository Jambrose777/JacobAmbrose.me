World Cube Association

WCA is a non-profit organization that governs twisty-puzzle (like a Rubik's Cube) competitions. It's mission is to have more competitions in more countries with more people and more fun, under fair and equal conditions.
In 2010, I began my speedcubing career by attending my first competition in Lexington, Kentucky. Though I was not the fastest, I did meet a lot of my cubing idols and had a great experience. I soon met more cubers in my local area and built life-long friendships that are irreplacable.
WCA is a completely volunteer-based organization. Everyone from a competitor to the board members all volunteer into their roles. In 2015, I organized my first competition to help give back to the community. I soon became more engrossed into the WCA and became a valued member of the WCA.
