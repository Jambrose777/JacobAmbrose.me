A WCA Delegate oversees a WCA competition to ensure that the competition is run in accordance to WCA's mission, spirit, and Regulations of the WCA. Delegates are the officials that make rulings on incidents. Delegates work with organizers to ensure that competitions are held with high quality. Delegates are also responsible for compiling and submitting Results and a competition Report. We help organizers create a feasible schedule for the competition, create a budget, and give advise on what to do the day of the competition.

CubingUSA Southeast Championship 2022

Gatlinburg, TN
July 2022
Every year, the Southeast Delegates organize a Regional Championship for the Southeast. This year the competition was held in Gatlinburg, Tennessee. This was the biggest and greatest Southeast Competition we have ever held. The competition was 3 days long, had 274 Competitors, and around 800 attendees. We held all 17 official WCA events as well as 4 extra events. We awarded the Top-3 Podium, Southeast Champion, and 6 State Champions in each event. This competition had the most results acheived out of any non-national/contential/world championship with over 14,000 solves completed.
